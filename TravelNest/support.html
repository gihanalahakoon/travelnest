Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track visited destinations? +

Head to the Travel Mood page. Each destination card has a "Mark Visited" and a "Plan Trip" button. Your selections are saved in your browser automatically.

Where is my data stored? +

All data — including saved budgets, your wishlist, newsletter email, and feedback — is stored in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to an external server.

Can I suggest a new destination? +

Absolutely. Use the feedback form on this page to share your suggestion and we will consider adding it in a future update.

Does TravelNest work offline? +

TravelNest is a Progressive Web App (PWA). Once you've visited the site, core assets are cached by the service worker, so the site will continue to work without an internet connection.

How does the Random Trip Generator work? +

The generator filters destinations from the built-in dataset based on the travel type and budget range you select, then randomly picks one from the matching results.
